# Bombs and Castles – Features Roadmap

## Long-Term Game Mechanism Features

1. **Advanced Battle Mechanics**
   - Special abilities for soldiers/towers
   - Power-ups and upgrades
   - Dynamic weather or terrain effects

2. **Strategic Defense & Village Expansion**
   - Customizable layouts
   - Resource management (gold, wood, etc.)
   - Building upgrades and unlocks

3. **Multiplayer Enhancements**
   - Matchmaking and lobbies
   - Player profiles, rankings, statistics
   - Clan/guild system

4. **UI/UX & Visuals**
   - Animated transitions, effects
   - Responsive/touch-optimized controls
   - Custom skins/themes

5. **Sound & Music**
   - Background music
   - Sound effects for actions/events
   - Voice chat (optional)

6. **Testing & Security**
   - Automated tests (unit, integration)
   - Anti-cheat mechanisms
   - Authentication and authorization

7. **Performance & Optimization**
   - Asset compression
   - Network optimization
   - Database scaling

8. **Release & Live Operations**
   - In-app purchases
   - Analytics and telemetry
   - Live events and updates
